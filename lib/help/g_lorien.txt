#####R === Lorien ===

Lorien is the god of sleep.

#####GThe benefits of Worshipping Lorien
 1. Lorien likes it if you kill monsters who resist sleep.
 2. As you increase your piety, Lorien will eventually grant
    free action and resistances to confusion, force and dark.
 3. He grants an intelligence bonus if you're pious enough and praying.

#####GThe disadvantages of Worshipping Lorien
1. Your piety decreases over time, especially when praying.
2. He dislikes it if you burn or destroy trees with magic or allow monsters to
   do so.
3. He will completely abandon you if you wear The One Ring.
~~~~~03|Lorien|Prayers
#####GLorien's Magic
Worshipping Lorien gives the adventurer access to a set of special
spells that come directly from the hands of Lorien. These spells use your piety
to cast rather than your spellpoints, and the level of spells that Lorien will 
permit you to use is determined by your Prayer skill - how skillful you are
in asking for his help without offending him!

There is a special book called the "Tome of Drowsiness" which 
contains instructions for the procedure for each of the prayers Lorien will
grant. There are three prayers all told, which are:
1. [[[[[BSleep Powder] (Level 1)
   Shoots a ball of sleep at monsters.
2. [[[[[BCollusion] (Level 15)
   Creates a cloud of darkness.
   The cloud will persist for some turns, damaging all monsters passing by.
3. [[[[[BTime Out] (Level 30)
   Creates walls around you.
   
Each of these spells can be increased in level both by improving your Prayer
skill, and by improving your Spell-power skill.

In addition to his specific magic, Lorien will also assist with your ability to
use some magic from the "standard" schools, in relation to how skilled you 
are at Prayer. These schools are as follows:
    *****m_nature.txt*0[Nature School] at 1/3 the Prayer skill level.
    *****m_celest.txt*0[Celestial School] at 1/3 the Prayer skill level.
    *****m_mind.txt*0[Mind School] at 1/2 the Prayer skill level.
    *****m_music.txt*0[Music] at 1/2 the Prayer skill level.

The spells from these schools are all cast using your normal spellpoints.
