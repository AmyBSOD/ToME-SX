#####R === Nessa ===

Nessa is the goddess of speed and joy.

#####GThe benefits of Worshipping Nessa
 1. Nessa likes it if you kill eldritch horrors.
 2. Nessa likes it if you kill chaos-based monsters or ones that resist
    every regular element.
 3. As your piety increases, your chance to dodge will go up.
 4. If you have a lot of piety, praying to her grants free action.

#####GThe disadvantages of Worshipping Nessa
1. Your piety decreases over time.
2. She will completely abandon you if you wear The One Ring.

#####GNessa's Magic
Worshipping Nessa gives the adventurer access to a set of special
spells that come directly from the hands of Nessa. These spells use your piety
to cast rather than your spellpoints, and the level of spells that Nessa will 
permit you to use is determined by your Prayer skill - how skillful you are
in asking for her help without offending her!

There is a special book called the "Tome of Joy" which 
contains instructions for the procedure for each of the prayers Nessa will
grant. There are three prayers all told, which are:
1. [[[[[BConfusion Fu] (Level 1)
   Imbues your melee weapon with confusion damage.
2. [[[[[BSpeedboost] (Level 15)
   Speeds you up for a few turns.
3. [[[[[BMana Storm] (Level 30)
   Casts a powerful ball of mana that can't be resisted.
   
Each of these spells can be increased in level both by improving your Prayer
skill, and by improving your Spell-power skill.

In addition to her specific magic, Nessa will also assist with your ability to
use some magic from the "standard" schools, in relation to how skilled you 
are at Prayer. These schools are as follows:
    *****m_nature.txt*0[Nature School] at 2/5 the Prayer skill level.
    *****m_convey.txt*0[Conveyance School] at 1/2 the Prayer skill level.
    *****m_geoman.txt*0[Geomancy] at 1/3 the Prayer skill level.

The spells from these schools are all cast using your normal spellpoints.
