#####R === ToME Magic - Elemental School ===

The elemental school of magic contains spells which fire elemental
blasts at the enemy. There are rumors of a "Tome of Elemental Magic"
which contains all the elemental school spells within its bindings.

#####sElemental Spells
There are five spells available for the elemental school. These spells are:
1. [[[[[sFire Lightning] (school level 1)
   Shoots a bolt that hits a single target.
   It randomly deals either fire or lightning damage.
2. [[[[[sCold Ball] (school level 5)
   Shoots a ball of cold at the enemy.
   At level 40 it deals ice damage instead.
3. [[[[[sAcid Rain] (school level 10)
   Creates a cloud of acid.
   The cloud will persist for some turns, damaging all monsters passing by.
4. [[[[[sLightning Bolt] (school level 15)
   Shoots a lightning bolt from your fingertips.
   It hits all monsters in a straight line.
5. [[[[[sEther Spread] (school level 40)
   Fills the air with ether to continuously damage enemies.
